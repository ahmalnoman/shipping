Our Legacy

Built on
eleven decades
of the sea

SBH Shipping Co. was born from a family of maritime professionals who have navigated the world's most critical trade routes since 1914. What started as a regional agency grew into a multi-corridor logistics group with partnerships across the Arabian Peninsula and the Horn of Africa.

In 2026, we launch our new Egypt headquarters — bringing together everything we've built into one unified brand, one platform, and one commitment: to move what matters, without fail.

Unmatched regional expertise

Deep knowledge of Red Sea, Suez, and Gulf of Aden corridors — built over 112 years, not learned from a manual.

Genuine partnerships

Egypt's strategic position

1914
Founded in Egypt
First maritime agency established in Alexandria, focused on ship husbandry and crew services.
1952
Dubai partnership formed
Established freight forwarding partnership in Jebel Ali, opening routes to Southeast Asia and the Indian subcontinent.
1989
Horn of Africa expansion
Opened offices in Djibouti and Aden — establishing control over the full Red Sea and Gulf of Aden corridor.

Merged all regional operations into a single logistics platform — FCL, LCL, project cargo, and inland distribution unified.
